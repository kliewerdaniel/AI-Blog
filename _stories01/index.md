**Key Elements:**

- **Objective:** Searching for Zora.

**Analysis:**

### Segment 2:

- **Objective:** Searching for El Dorado.
- **Setting:** Dense Amazonian forest.

### Segment 3:
**Key Elements:**

- **Objective:** Searching for El Dorado.
- **Setting:** Dense Amazonian forest.
